Welcome to Godrej Reserve

Godrej Reserve is an expansive mixed-use project in Kandivali, offering spacious 2, 3 and 4 BHK apartments with balconies and commercial shop spaces. Experience resort-style living as the project features a grand clubhouse, a 2.5-acre retreat with lush gardens, along with 25+ lifestyle amenities like a gym, squash court, swimming pool, amphitheater, jogging track, salon, cricket field, etc. Spring terraces, green open spaces, etc. The project has commercial shops on the ground and first-floor levels. The commercial space offers 45 shops for retail usage. This attracts more investors looking for a functional and well-connected mixed-use development.

Kandivali is well-equipped with popular schools, hospitals, malls and recreational spots including Sharda Gyanpeeth School, ESIS Hospital, Growel’s Mall, and Oberoi Mall within the proximity of the project. Accessibility to Kandivali Railway Station, Goregaon Metro Station, Aarey Road, Unit No 13 Bus Stop, and the Western Express Highway makes commuting easy. About Godrej Properties

Founded in 1897, Godrej Properties is a leading real estate developer in India with a range of residential townships, business parks, and mixed-use properties as part of its portfolio. The firm's properties stand out for their lovely architecture, quality residences, tranquil lush environs, and use of cutting-edge technologies. With a grand legacy of 125 years in the real estate industry, Godrej Properties has changed the skyline of many cities in the country.
